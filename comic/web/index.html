COMIC — quick guide

A 3D glass-brain figure maker. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Load data

  • + map — load NIfTI volumes (.nii/.nii.gz) in MNI152 space, or fsaverage surface maps (.gii/.mgh/.mgz; drop lh+rh together). Drag onto the canvas or use the button.
  • Demo — load example Neurosynth maps.
  • threshold — voxels below |value| are dropped when the map is meshed (set it before loading).

Each overlay (one row)

  • Colormap swatch + auto / sequential / diverging scale.
  • blocky / smooth / surface — voxel cubes, isosurface, or projected onto the cortex.
  • thr statistical threshold · k cluster-extent (hide clusters < k voxels) · colour limits, gamma, veil, edges.
  • 👁 hide · ✕ remove · drag the name to reorder (later overlays draw on top).

Global look

  • Inflate pial↔inflated cortex · Surface project all maps onto the cortex · Outline the cortex lines.
  • cortex α glass opacity · edge thr / line w cortex-line density & thickness · vox α line strength over voxels.
  • Cut MRI — on a sliced panel, reveal the anatomical T1 cross-section on the cut face. Cut map composites thresholded NIfTI values over it; slab thickness and linear/nearest sampling are explicit.

Layouts

  • layout dropdown — a built-in arrangement (free canvas, lateral/medial, 5- or 6-view montage, 8-panel) or save/load your own to this browser.

Free Canvas

  • Hover a panel for its toolbar: view picker · ✂ slice (cycles axial/coronal/sagittal cut, then sphere & cube bite) · ☰ more (rotate steps, cortex lines, bring-to-front, remove).
  • Drag the brain to move · use the bottom-right red X / green Y / blue Z gizmo for precise MNI-axis rotation (click an endpoint to snap) · drag the square corner to resize. Shift-drag remains a free-orbit shortcut.
  • With a slice on: drag the orange/teal dots to move/resize the cut, and toggle Cut MRI to see the anatomy inside.
  • Toolbar: Seed grid · + panel · Reset · transparent background. Cmd/Ctrl-Z undoes edits.

Export

  • Save brain full-res PNG · Save bars the colorbars · GIF a spinning turntable · Colorbar show/hide.
  • Copy CLI — a comic render command (or a figure.json) that reproduces the exact view from the command line.
